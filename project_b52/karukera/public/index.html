Villa Karukera

A vacation made for you

Welcome to villa Karukera

Our house is located directly at the sea in mediterranean pearl Rovanjska, 20 km northeast from Zadar. Just a few minutes ride from the highway to get to us and there are some restaurants, bars and a shop nearby.

Sunsets to remember

Whether its from the open interior, outdoor pool area or the rooftop jacuzzi & lounge space, the sunsets in Rovanjska are simply unforgettable. Furthermore, if swimming isn't enough exercise for you, we've included an inside gym.

Fairytale daytrips

Some of the worlds greatest sights, at the palm of your hand. Enjoy Maslenica bridge bungee, Zrmanja canyon, mighty Velebit mountain, and a total of five national parks at your disposal.
